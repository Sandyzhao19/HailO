# Hail-O Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 22, 2026

## Data Collection
Hail-O collects minimal data to function:
- **Location Data:** Used only to filter relevant weather warnings. Not stored permanently or shared with third parties.
- **User Preferences:** Your chosen monitoring location is stored locally in your browser.

## Data Usage
- Location data is used solely to determine which weather warnings are relevant to you
- All weather data comes directly from the Australian Bureau of Meteorology (bom.gov.au)
- No personal information is collected, transmitted, or sold

## Third-Party Services
Hail-O fetches data from:
- Australian Bureau of Meteorology (www.bom.gov.au) - for official weather warnings

## Data Storage
- Your location preference is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No tracking or analytics are used
